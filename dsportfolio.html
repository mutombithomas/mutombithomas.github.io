Novelty Detection of Jet Engine Data

My master’s thesis focused on the classification problem of novelty detection of Rolls Royce jet engine data, and the attempts made in finding a solution to industry supplied data. The problem is motivated by non-critical maintenance tests where the automatic detection of faulty and normally operating engines would provide industry with a method for efficient diagnosis of jet engine behaviour.

For the purposes of confidentiality, the majority of the report has been redacted and the following only provides an overview of the methods used.

COVID-19 in Africa

Tableau dashboard demonstration that highlights the misleading nature of COVID-19 deaths in developing countries.

It is known that Africa as a continent has lower quality healthcare infrastructure and resources available compared to economically developed continents. With this in mind, it's surprising that Africa has only recorded 202,000 deaths since the start of the pandemic. It's evident that COVID-19 death statistics don't accurately reflect the impact of the virus on the contient due to a number of factors. The extent of these unreported deaths are highlighted in Tableau, along with examples of exceptions to the trend.
